Privacy Policy

mi.show (owned by MS Media LLC, a California Limited Liability Company) is a subscription content and freelance marketplace platform, accessible by any users over the age of 18. This privacy policy applies to all subscribers, freelance creators, and users of the website. By accessing our website you agree to this privacy policy as well as our Terms of Use.

Information that we collect

Regardless of account type: subscriber or creator, we collect this basic information at signup:

  1. Your e-mail
  2. Username
  3. Password

You must sign up for an account to utilize our service. During your first login, you will be asked what type of account you are signing up for: creator or subscriber. The subscriber account is purely for subscribing to freelance creators, browsing submitted content, products, services and interacting with creators via comments and messages.

You may also sign up via social media (Google, Twitter, Instagram, etc.) in which we will ask permission to access basic information such as your name, email, profile and/or header images. You can manage the information you share with us via these platforms by going to their respective privacy/security options to disable sharing of any specific or all information.

Additionally, you can choose to add other data such as location, display name, website, etc., which will be stored similarly to comments.

Freelance Creators

A Freelance Creator is someone who wishes to utilize the platform to sell their creative works, products, and/or services. To become a creator, you must select the "I am a creator" option after you login for the first time. At that point, you can begin uploading content in your free tier. However, to receive payouts you must complete the Partner Agreement form which includes collecting additional information and you must agree to the agreement. Depending on your location, which is derived from your IP address, you must provide some or all of the following:

  • Legal Name or Company Name
  • Address and Country
  • Social media usernames (optional)
  • Date of birth
  • Tax Identification number or SSN (for IRS Form W-9)
  • Government Issued Identification Card (Drivers License, Passport, etc.)
  • A picture of you holding your ID
  • Banking information for payouts (depending on payout method selected)

All Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is encrypted using AES 256-bit encryption. Other information that you provide via profile settings and any information included in your posts, are designed to be posted and available to your subscribers and users of the website. Utilize tier subscription settings to control where and how your posts are shown to subscribers. You may also use privacy settings to set your profile to private.

Subscribers

A subscriber is a user who wishes to subscribe and/or purchase creative works, products, and/or services. We collect your e-mail, username, and password during sign up. You may also enter in additional information that identifies you as a user, such as: display name, profile picture, location, and date of birth to ensure you are over the age of 18.

Additional information that we collect as you use our services

We will never sell or share your information with any entity, company, or person. We utilize third party solutions to ensure our service is running smoothly. Additional information that we gather include your browser type, access times, pages viewed and your IP address. Some actions, such as subscriptions and purchases are inherently recorded in our system and are displayed to only the appropriate users (i.e., the user(s) involved and admins).

Third-Party Payment Processors

All payments are performed using third party services. We do not record or store any payment information that you submit.

The Privacy Mission

At the core of what we do, we value and respect your right to privacy. As we continue to grow, we will introduce new ways of controlling your privacy in as many aspects as deemed appropriate by us and the community (all of you!). We thank you for using our service. You receive notification via e-mail and/or platform notification any time this policy is updated.

Our Take Down Process

Upon receipt of a take down request, website administrators of mi.show will expeditiously flag the content for review. During this review process, content will be unavailable for retrieval unless otherwise determined. As indicated in our Terms of Service under Content Policy, we reserve the right to remove any content, material, information, and/or data at any time for any reason. It is also the policy of this website to terminate the account of repeat offenders and make every attempt to prevent them from accessing the site again via permanent ban (“permaban”). The affected user may choose to submit a counter-notification to the designated agent containing a statement under penalty of perjury that the user (creator) believes in good faith that the work cited for take down was improperly identified. The user may further submit all materials/content used in the creation of said content. After the notification has been received by our designated agent, the content will be restored in full within 10 business days unless formal court action has been filed by the party or representative who submitted the original notice of copyright infringement.

DMCA Counter-Notification Process

If you are the owner of the content that has been removed, you will have received an e-mail of the notice of take down from the e-mail address listed under Notice of Copyright Infringement. We have made the counter-notification process as simple as possible. Reply to the e-mail with the following information:

  • Include this statement in the document:

    • “I [insert your full legal name], under penalty of perjury, hereby declare that under good faith the referenced material in the ‘notice of copyright infringement’ information was mistakenly identified and removed temporarily.”
    • Include confirmation that the material identified was posted by you and that the system did not notify you by mistake;
    • A statement that the user will accept any legal process from your country of origin or residence, whether the United States or other territory that recognizes the DMCA or equivalent, from the person who initiated the claim.
    • Include all information above in a generated PDF that contains the electronic signature of the user.
    • Send attachment in reply to the e-mail that you received.
  • Your reply must be in English or Spanish. No other languages will be accepted in your reply and will not be processed.

As indicated above, content will be restored unless otherwise contested in a formal court process within 10 business days.
